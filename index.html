Research Areas

Bioluminescence

Bioluminescence

Bioluminescent enzymes are ubiquitous in sea life, and are hugely useful in biotechnology. We are developing new ways to use these enzymes for measuring interactions in and between our cells. Because these enzymes emit light as a result of a chemical reaction, they enable us to detect cellular events with high sensitivity!

Machine Learning

Machine Learning

We use machine learning (essentially fancy stats) to gain insight into the fundamental chemical interactions in our enzymes and proteins. The protein-binder interaction data that we gather in the lab is analyzed is modeled in Rosetta and analyzed with python!

Protein Engineering

Protein Engineering

We use random mutations to tune the functions of proteins. This enables us to change the brightness or color of a bioluminescent protein, or change the selectivity of a protein-peptide interaction!
